I need to write something everyday to improve my English, and further more, to defeat my endless loneliness.

Start with a poem translated by ChatGPT(I’ll continue changing it till it can’t be better.):

I harbor a dream, wild and free, unashamed to declare it to all I see:
A longing for life, boundless and grand,
An eternal dance in time’s shifting sand.

I adore this world, in its endless embrace,
The warm sun’s caress, the moon’s silent grace.
I cherish the spring’s awakening breeze,
The summer’s fiery zest, autumn rains that tease,
The winter’s pure snow, a canvas so bright,
In each fleeting moment, in day and in night.

I revel in chance, in destiny’s play,
In the fierce battles with fate’s powerful sway.
I love the souls, scattered and near,
United in struggle, in hope, and in fear.
Each second, each minute, in this world so dear,
Holds perfection, a longing, a reason to cheer.

So much to do, so much to see,
To savor each flavor, each mystery.
To seek the truths of science, profound and clear,
In its laws and wonders, ever so near.
To wander through landscapes, breathtaking and vast,
To delve into works profound and vast.
To thank all who’ve helped, to extend a hand,
To those in need, in this wondrous land.

Oh, for a life unbound by time,
A world of possibilities, sublime.
No more despair, no anxious tide,
Like the eternal cosmos, undefined.
In such a life, no need to ponder,
The meaning of existence, to wander.

Yet, I know, of life, of mind, of thought,
We understand little, though we’ve sought.
Infinite life, a fantasy so bold,
A sci-fi dream, in our hearts, it’s told.
That’s why my passion for research burns bright,
For understanding these phenomena, day and night.

To unravel the codes of life, of intelligence, of thought,
To dream of a day when we’re bound no more
To our mortal shells, but possess a form
More powerful, enduring, through every storm.

Thus, I’ll pursue knowledge, in every way,
To explore further, to seek, to find,
The secrets of life, intelligence, and mind.

Life, mind, intelligence,
One day, I’ll uncover your essence, your quintessence!
